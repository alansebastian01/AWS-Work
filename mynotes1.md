### Quick AWS Exam Tip

For AWS:

* **RDS** supports relational databases such as **MySQL**, **PostgreSQL**, and **Oracle**.
* **DynamoDB** is **NoSQL**, not relational.
* **Redshift** is a data warehouse used for analytics and reporting.
