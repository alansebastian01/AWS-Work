Yes. AWS updated the **Cloud Adoption Framework (AWS CAF)** from the older **6 Perspectives** model to a newer **6 Perspectives / 47 Capabilities** model. Many certification questions now use the newer capability names.

## AWS CAF (Current Version)

### 1. Business Perspective

Focus: Business outcomes and value realization.

Examples of capabilities:

* Strategy Management
* Product Management
* Business Insights
* Data Science
* Data Monetization
* Innovation Management

---

### 2. People Perspective

Focus: Organization, culture, workforce, and change.

Examples of capabilities:

* Organizational Alignment
* Organization Design
* Workforce Transformation
* Talent Management
* Leadership

---

### 3. Governance Perspective

Focus: Financial governance, risk, and investment management.

Examples of capabilities:

* Cloud Financial Management
* Benefits Management
* Risk Management
* Portfolio Management
* Program and Project Management

---

### 4. Platform Perspective

Focus: Building and operating cloud platforms.

Examples of capabilities:

* Platform Architecture
* Platform Engineering
* Data Architecture
* Data Engineering
* Modern Application Development

---

### 5. Security Perspective

Focus: Security, compliance, and protection.

Examples of capabilities:

* Identity and Access Management
* Threat Detection
* Security Assurance
* Incident Response
* Infrastructure Protection
* Application Security

---

### 6. Operations Perspective

Focus: Running, monitoring, and improving workloads.

Examples of capabilities:

* Observability
* Event Management
* Incident and Problem Management
* Change and Release Management
* Service Management
* Business Continuity and Disaster Recovery (BCDR)

---
