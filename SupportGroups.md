## AWS Support Questions Found in Your ZIP (with Answers & Tips)

### Q71

**A company wants to assess its operational readiness and mitigate risks before a product launch. Which AWS Support plan offers guidance and support for this event at no additional charge?**

A. AWS Business Support
B. AWS Basic Support
C. AWS Developer Support
✅ **D. AWS Enterprise Support**

**Tip:** Operational reviews, launch guidance, and proactive event support = **Enterprise Support**.

---

### Q135

**A company is considering switching from AWS Business Support to AWS Enterprise Support. Which additional benefit will it receive?**

A. Full Trusted Advisor checks
B. 24/7 phone, email, and chat support
✅ **C. A designated Technical Account Manager (TAM)**
D. Architecture guidance

**Tip:** Business Support already includes 24/7 support and Trusted Advisor. The big differentiator is **TAM**.

---

### Q236

**At what support level do users receive access to a Support Concierge?**

A. Basic Support
B. Developer Support
C. Business Support
✅ **D. Enterprise Support**

**Tip:** **Support Concierge** = billing/account assistance = Enterprise.

### Q265

**How does the AWS Enterprise Support Concierge team help users?**

A. Supporting application development
B. Providing architecture guidance
✅ **C. Answering billing and account inquiries**
D. Answering technical support questions

**Tip:** Concierge handles **billing and account issues**, not technical troubleshooting.

### Q556

### Q602

**A company runs production workloads in AWS. Which AWS Support plan meets these requirements at the LOWEST cost?**

✅ **A. Developer** *(according to the question set's accepted answer)*
B. Enterprise On-Ramp
